    \Huge{Ehangu}\\
    \vspace{1cm}\\
    \normalsize{
      \noindent
      Mae adran 'Ehangu' wedi'i dylunio ar gyfer y rhai sy'n ceisio ehangu eu gorwelion y tu hwnt i'r repertoire craidd. Yma fe welwch gasgliad o alawon llai cyffredin ac yn aml yn fwy heriol, wedi'u curadu'n benodol i wthio ffiniau offerynwyr. Mae'r alawon hyn yn mynnu mwy o fedrusrwydd technegol a dehongliad cerddorol, gan gynnig profiad gwerth chweil i'r rhai sy'n barod i archwilio cymhlethdodau newydd a chyfoethogi eu dealltwriaeth o gerddoriaeth draddodiadol.
    }

    \newcolumn

    \Huge{Expansion}\\
    \vspace{1cm}\\
    \normalsize{
      \noindent
      The 'Expansion' section is designed for those seeking to broaden their horizons beyond the core repertoire. Here you'll find a collection of less common and often more challenging melodies, specifically curated to push the boundaries for instrumentalists. These tunes demand greater technical proficiency and musical interpretation, offering a rewarding experience for those ready to explore new complexities and enrich their understanding of traditional music.
    }
